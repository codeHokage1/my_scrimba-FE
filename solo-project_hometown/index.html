Top 3 things to do in Lagos!

ART

Some notable places to explore include the Nike Art Gallery, Terra Kulture, and the National Museum Lagos. These venues offer a unique experience to learn about the rich cultural heritage of Nigeria and its impact on contemporary African art. Check this out.

NATURE

One of the most popular spots is Lekki Conservation Centre, a vast nature reserve that's home to a wide range of wildlife, including monkeys, crocodiles, and over 200 species of birds. Visitors can enjoy walking on the 1.8 km long boardwalk that takes you through the forest, or take part in a tree-top canopy walk for an even more thrilling experience. Check this out.

MUSIC

The home of Burna Boy, Wizkid, Tems and many more! With a thriving music scene that encompasses everything from traditional folk music to modern Afrobeat, Lagos has something for everyone. Visitors can take in live performances at venues like Freedom Park and Terra Kulture or explore the city's many record stores and music festivals.
